About Google Ads Exact Match Formatter

When planning your PPC campaigns, using the Google Ads Keyword Planner exact match type is essential for budgeting and precise targeting. However, formatting lists manually can be time-consuming. Our free online tool acts as a dedicated keyword planner exact match wrapper, allowing you to convert raw search terms into square brackets instantly.

Simply paste your raw keyword research, use the core operations to clean up special characters or remove duplicates, and get perfectly formatted phrases ready for your Google Ads account. No installation, no registration—just pure utility for agile marketers.

Pro Tip: Pressing Convert to Exact Match automatically trims spaces, avoids double-brackets, and preserves spaces within phrases!
